There is no right way to use this project. Here are a few of the resources/interactivites available on the site:

  • Click on a land area on the map to see all flood myths from that area.
  • Hover over the pins on the map to see specific region/country information.
  • Click on a pin to see flood myths from that country.
  • To see all of the data, visit the database.
  • For more information on the data, visit the Data tab.

Have fun exploring!
